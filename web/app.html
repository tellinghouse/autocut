Drop in your camera angles. It syncs them, figures out who's talking, and cuts the show for you.

1 What are we making?

Drag & drop your camera / mic files here, or click to browse
Each camera's own video file is best. You can also add separate mic/recorder audio files.
Resume a previous session
Every finished cut is saved as a project automatically. Open one to keep editing by transcript, add branding, or re-download files.
Batch a whole folder (overnight mode)
Point AutoCut at a folder on this computer. Each subfolder that contains a video becomes one episode, using its files and the folder's name. Files are read in place — no uploading.
Leave the console window open. You can close the browser — progress picks up when you come back.

★ Intro, outro & titles

They're automatically resized and joined onto the episode. Timestamps in the transcript, captions, and chapters shift to match.
Name card = lower-third with name and title that fades in and out. Banner = centered strip near the bottom (great for "Subscribe" or "Thanks to our sponsor"). Times are in the current video's clock.

✎ Edit by transcript

Click a line's time or speaker to strike it out (click again to restore) — struck lines get cut from the video, along with the dead time after them. Shift-click marks a whole range. Click the text to fix typos (text fixes update the transcript files, not the video).
