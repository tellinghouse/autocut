What it does

An editor that already knows the plan.

Drop in your footage and AutoCut handles the tedious parts — the syncing, the cutting, the captions — so you can focus on the conversation.

MULTICAMSyncs every angle

Cameras and mics that started at different moments are lined up to one timeline automatically, by matching the audio they all picked up.

THE CUTFollows the conversation

The edit switches to whoever's speaking, with a minimum shot length so it never flickers between cameras.

SOLOTightens talking heads

Single-camera video? AutoCut trims the dead air into clean jump cuts and levels your audio to podcast loudness.

WORDSTranscript, captions & chapters

A timestamped transcript, an .srt caption file, and paste-ready YouTube chapters — all generated on your machine.

SOCIALFinds your clips

Detects the highest-energy moments and cuts them as vertical 9:16 shorts for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

CONTROLEdit by transcript

Delete a line of dialogue and the video re-cuts to match. Hand off a project file to Premiere, Resolve, or Final Cut anytime.

Getting started

Installed in under a minute.

It installs like any normal program — a Start-menu entry, a desktop icon, and a clean uninstaller.

Download

Grab AutoCut-Setup.exe using the button below.

Run it

Double-click the installer and follow the prompts.

Launch

Open AutoCut from your desktop icon; it opens in your browser.

Make video

Drop in footage, click once, and let it work.

A quick heads-up on first launch

Because AutoCut is a small independent tool, Windows may show a blue "Windows protected your PC" screen the first time you run it. That's expected — click More info, then Run anyway. It's completely safe.
